HOPE (by 'pd singh')
FACEBOOKINSTAGRAMTWITTER

manifestation of
free mentality,
a voice of heart
that was meant to be free;
not a strangled pigeon;
crying,
helpless,
unsure of help!

thoughts that weren't
restricted to cottage houses
filled with discarded furniture
and untidy floors;
broken crockery
and noisy doors;
no electricity and weak walls,
no water and no well-functioning clocks!

my glimmer has strengthened again
as i shine in this darkness
of void individuals,
people with dead brains
and caged souls,
killing everyone who seems
senseful and positive;
differences are judged,
happiness is cringed upon,
love is criminilized,
and peace is tagged as pollution,
and freedom a poison!

but my glimmer won't fade,
neither my revolt for freedom;
from the depth of darkness
i will rise,
from the depth of ignorance
i will rise,
because this is what human was
meant to be
THE ONE
TRUE FREE BEING!
